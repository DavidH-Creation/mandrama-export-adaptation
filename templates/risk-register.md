## Notes

- **Cultural misunderstanding**: Scenes or references that international audiences may misread, find offensive, or simply not understand
- **Pacing**: Episodes that are too slow, too exposition-heavy, or structured in ways that don't match short-form retention patterns
- **Dubbing**: Lines that are difficult to perform naturally in the target language, lip-sync challenges, accent/register issues
- **Visual format**: Uncanny valley risk, production quality inconsistency, format-tone mismatch
- **Sponsorability**: Content that makes brand integration difficult — violence level, sexual content, controversial themes, ethical gray areas
