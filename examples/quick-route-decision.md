# Example: Quick Route Decision

## User Ask

```text
Should this Chinese short drama launch English-led, Spanish-led, or subtitle-first?

Premise:
- A disinherited heiress discovers her fiance and cousin framed her
- She enters a strategic marriage with a ruthless founder to retake her family company
- Tone: glossy revenge melodrama

Constraints:
- Fast turnaround
- AI-assisted production
- Goal is YouTube growth first, sponsors later
```

## Example Output

### 1. Decision Snapshot

```text
- Verdict: Anglophone premium export
- Confidence: Medium
- Best market: US / UK YouTube short-drama audience
- Core reason: The premise is globally legible because betrayal, humiliation, and comeback travel well. English-led packaging gives the best first shot at discoverability and future sponsor upside, while subtitle-first release would make the project feel more imported than positioned.
```

### 2. Reasoning Table

| Signal | Type | Confidence | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|---|
| Revenge and status-reversal hooks are already strong | fact | high | The skill does not need to invent a new emotional engine |
| Luxury family-business stakes can be rewritten into global corporate power logic | inference | medium | Makes Anglophone packaging viable without losing the core fantasy |
| Fast-turnaround YouTube distribution favors the broadest discovery language first | inference | medium | Supports English-led launch despite higher dubbing burden |
| No existing LATAM distribution partner was mentioned | assumption | low | Weakens the case for Spanish-led launch as the first move |

### 3. Ranked Options

1. **Recommended option:** Anglophone premium export
   Why it wins: Best balance of reach, monetization upside, and package clarity for this premise.
2. **Second-best option:** Region-first Spanish-led LATAM launch
   Why it loses: Could work if a dubbing partner or channel advantage exists, but that advantage is not yet established.
3. **Reject for now:** Subtitle-first imported release
   Why it is weaker: Leaves too much culture-friction and lowers perceived package quality for a revenge-luxury premise.

### 4. Immediate Next Move

- What to test next: Rewrite the logline and title set for English-first discovery
- What to rewrite next: Convert family-pressure logic into inheritance-control and board-politics logic
- What the user must confirm: Whether LATAM, MENA, or another region has a real distribution edge
