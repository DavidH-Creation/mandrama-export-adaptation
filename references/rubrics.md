# Scoring Rubric

Score each adaptation from 1-5 on these 9 dimensions. Any score of 2 or below must trigger a rewrite note explaining what needs to change.

| # | Dimension | 1 (Fail) | 3 (Acceptable) | 5 (Strong) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Export legibility** | Requires Chinese cultural knowledge to follow | Mostly clear, minor opaque moments | Fully legible to international audience |
| 2 | **Emotional retention** | Core emotional engine is lost | Emotional core preserved but diluted | Emotional engine is intact and amplified |
| 3 | **Language-market fit** | Language package mismatches target market | Language works but not optimized | Language package maximizes monetization |
| 4 | **Dialogue naturalness** | Sounds translated | Functional but not memorable | Native speakers would assume it was written in target language |
| 5 | **Production feasibility** | Beyond team capability or budget | Achievable with effort | Well-matched to pipeline and resources |
| 6 | **Hook strength** | Weak openings, no cliffhangers | Adequate hooks for some episodes | Every episode has a strong open and close |
| 7 | **Sponsorability** | Sponsor-toxic content throughout | Some risk areas but manageable | Clean for premium brand integration |
| 8 | **Format coherence** | Visual format fights the story tone | Format works but isn't optimal | Format amplifies the story's strengths |
| 9 | **Packaging strength** | Generic titles and thumbnails | Serviceable packaging | Packaging is click-worthy and platform-optimized |

## Scoring Rules

- Score each dimension independently
- Provide a 1-sentence justification for each score
- Any dimension at **2 or below** requires a rewrite note with specific changes
- Overall export readiness = lowest score determines the bottleneck
- Target: all dimensions at 3+ before declaring the adaptation ready
